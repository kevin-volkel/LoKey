About LoKey

LoKey is an all-female law public safety team who believe that women are overlooked in the public safety field. We started our company to prove that women can do anything that a man can do and look good while doing it. It is run by four female highly trained professional women who had a dream to show what we are capable. Our company was started in 2020 and is ran by Yahaira Lopez, Mackenzie Evinger, Kimberly Cordova, and Michelle Fuentes.
